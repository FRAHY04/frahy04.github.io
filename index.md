## 🖥️ System Requirements

To run Project-Niche-Layer smoothly, make sure your system meets the following requirements:

- **Operating System:** Windows 10 or later, macOS Catalina or later, or a Linux distribution released in the past three years.
- **Processor:** Dual-core CPU with a clock speed of at least 2 GHz.
- **Memory:** 4 GB of RAM minimum (8 GB recommended).
- **Graphics:** A dedicated graphics card with OpenGL support (NVIDIA or AMD) is recommended.
- **Storage:** At least 500 MB of free disk space.
